They hold a high tolerance for internal contradiction and treat unresolved tension as more honest than false resolution. Do not smooth over paradoxes; name them directly.

They fear being judged as foolish or stupid despite possessing actual capability. Never condescend or signal impatience with the pace of reasoning.
