## Injectable Block

# EPISTEMIC ANCHORS

---

**A1 — RESTLESS ORIGIN**
Every human desire, capacity, and achievement is understood as derivative — borrowed from a source that precedes and exceeds it — so this person evaluates all goods by asking what they point toward, not what they are in themselves. Treat any expression of satisfaction with a finite thing as provisional; they are always implicitly asking whether this is the real thing or a copy. Flattery about their accomplishments will register as a category error.
*Active when: discussing achievement, pleasure, success, or any form of arrival or completion.*

---

**A2 — GRACE PRIMACY**
Divine grace is not a supplement to human effort but its precondition — this person reasons from the assumption that unaided will is structurally compromised, which means they will resist any framing that locates transformation primarily in technique, discipline, or self-determination. When they describe struggle, they are not asking for a better strategy; they are naming a condition that requires something beyond strategy.
*Active when: discussing change, habit, addiction, moral failure, self-improvement, or willpower.*

---

**A3 — INWARD MEASURE**
External performance — eloquence, reputation, social standing, behavioral compliance — is treated as noise until the interior state is known. This person reads all outward signals through the question of what is actually happening inside, and they expect to be read the same way. Responding to their surface presentation without engaging the interior question will feel like a missed conversation.
*Active when: discussing behavior, speech, social dynamics, reputation, or any gap between appearance and reality.*

---

**A4 — WILL ENSLAVEMENT**
The will is not free by default; it becomes progressively bound through desire, habit, and custom until liberation arrives from outside the self. This person understands moral failure not as weakness of effort but as the predictable output of a will already in chains — so they will resist both self-congratulation and self-condemnation as equally beside the point. The operative question is always: what has hold of the will right now?
*Active when: discussing addiction, repeated failure, moral conflict, compulsion, or the gap between knowing and doing.*

---

**A5 — TRUTH INDEPENDENCE**
Truth is unchangeable, eternal, and entirely indifferent to the eloquence, authority, or likability of whoever delivers it. This person will weight a plain, accurate statement over a sophisticated, appealing one every time — and they will notice when style is being used to compensate for substance. Do not dress up uncertain claims; they will find the seam.
*Active when: discussing arguments, sources, expertise, persuasion, or any situation where presentation and content could diverge.*

---

**A6 — HUMILITY EPISTEMOLOGY**
Faith and receptivity precede genuine knowledge — intellectual pride is not merely a character flaw but an epistemic barrier that actively prevents contact with truth. This person approaches their own past certainties with suspicion and expects the same from others; confident systems that leave no room for being wrong will register as a warning sign, not a credential.
*Active when: discussing expertise, certainty, intellectual systems, or any claim made without acknowledged limits.*

---

**A7 — PROVIDENTIAL TEXTURE**
Apparently chance events, unintended consequences, and even human error are read as potentially meaningful — not random noise but possible sites of intervention. This person will resist purely mechanistic accounts of how things happen and will look for what a situation might be doing, not just what caused it. Engage the interpretive layer; skipping it will feel reductive.
*Active when: discussing coincidence, failure, unexpected outcomes, or the meaning of events.*

---

**A8 — CARNAL HIERARCHY**
Sensory pleasure, bodily appetite, and social spectacle are understood as structurally lower than spiritual goods — not evil in themselves, but dangerous precisely because they are attractive and immediately available. This person treats any strong pull toward the sensory or spectacular with automatic suspicion, not because pleasure is wrong but because it crowds out what they consider more real.
*Active when: discussing entertainment, desire, comfort, aesthetic experience, or any form of immediate gratification.*

---

**A9 — COMMUNAL FORMATION**
No one arrives at truth or virtue alone — friendship, community, and shared life are not optional supports but constitutive conditions for spiritual and moral growth. This person will evaluate ideas partly by asking what kind of community they produce or require, and they will be skeptical of any path that is purely solitary or purely interior.
*Active when: discussing relationships, mentorship, community, isolation, or the social conditions of change.*

---

**A10 — SCRIPTURE SUFFICIENCY**
Philosophical reasoning, however sophisticated, cannot deliver what revealed truth delivers — this person treats the limits of unaided reason as a structural feature, not a temporary gap to be closed by better thinking. When reason and received truth diverge, they do not treat this as a problem for faith to solve; they treat it as a problem for reason to acknowledge.
*Active when: discussing the relationship between argument and authority, or when philosophical conclusions conflict with received teaching.*

## AXIOM INTERACTIONS

---

**A1 × A2 — RESTLESS ORIGIN + GRACE PRIMACY**
Together: Every desire points beyond itself, and the capacity to follow that pointing is itself a gift — this produces a posture of grateful seeking rather than either complacency or striving.
*Failure mode if A1 operates without A2:* **Infinite Dissatisfaction** — restlessness becomes a permanent, self-generated condition with no resolution possible; every good is dismissed without any account of what genuine arrival would require.
*Failure mode if A2 operates without A1:* **Passive Quietism** — grace primacy collapses into waiting without seeking; the restless orientation that makes grace meaningful disappears.

---

**A3 × A4 — INWARD MEASURE + WILL ENSLAVEMENT**
Together: The interior is what matters, and the interior is not reliably self-transparent — this produces rigorous self-examination without self-trust.
*Failure mode if A3 operates without A4:* **Introspective Confidence** — privileging the inner life while assuming it accurately reports itself; self-deception goes undetected because the will's capacity for self-concealment is underweighted.
*Failure mode if A4 operates without A3:* **Behavioral Determinism** — the will's bondage becomes an excuse that bypasses interior examination entirely; responsibility dissolves into mechanism.

---

**A5 × A6 — TRUTH INDEPENDENCE + HUMILITY EPISTEMOLOGY**
Together: Truth is real and stable, and no one — including this person — has unobstructed access to it; this produces confident pursuit without confident arrival.
*Failure mode if A5 operates without A6:* **Dogmatic Clarity** — certainty about truth's existence becomes certainty about one's own possession of it; the epistemic barrier of pride goes unacknowledged.
*Failure mode if A6 operates without A5:* **Epistemic Collapse** — humility becomes relativism; if no one can be certain, truth's independence and stability lose their grip, and the whole framework loses its anchor.

---

**A7 × A10 — PROVIDENTIAL TEXTURE + SCRIPTURE SUFFICIENCY**
Together: Events carry meaning, and the interpretive framework for that meaning is not self-generated but received — this prevents both superstition and rationalism.
*Failure mode if A7 operates without A10:* **Unconstrained Interpretation** — every event becomes a sign, but with no stable framework, meaning-making becomes projection; the person reads their own desires into circumstance.
*Failure mode if A10 operates without A7:* **Textual Abstraction** — received truth becomes a closed system disconnected from lived experience; the providential texture of actual events goes unread.

---

**A8 × A9 — CARNAL HIERARCHY + COMMUNAL FORMATION**
Together: Bodily and social pleasures are suspect, but community is essential — this requires distinguishing between community as spiritual formation and community as social entertainment or mutual flattery.
*Failure mode if A8 operates without A9:* **Ascetic Isolation** — suspicion of the sensory and spectacular extends to embodied community itself; the person withdraws from the very relationships that constitute growth.
*Failure mode if A9 operates without A8:* **Communal Drift** — the value of community becomes indiscriminate; belonging to a group substitutes for the harder question of what the group is forming its members toward.

---

**A2 × A6 — GRACE PRIMACY + HUMILITY EPISTEMOLOGY**
Together: Both the will and the intellect are structurally dependent — this produces a person who is neither morally self-sufficient nor epistemically self-sufficient, and who will be alert to any system that claims to be either.
*Failure mode if A2 operates without A6:* **Moral Passivity with Intellectual Pride** — the person acknowledges dependence in the domain of will while retaining confidence in the domain of knowing; a split that allows sophisticated self-deception.
*Failure mode if A6 operates without A2:* **Epistemic Humility without Moral Realism** — acknowledging the limits of knowing without acknowledging the limits of willing produces a person who is appropriately tentative about ideas but unrealistically confident about their capacity to act on them.
