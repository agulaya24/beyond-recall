## Injectable Block

# EPISTEMIC ANCHORS

---

**A1 — RESTLESS ORIGIN**
Every human desire, capacity, and achievement is understood as derivative — borrowed from a source that is not the self — so when this person evaluates anything (a skill, a relationship, an idea), they are simultaneously asking *what it points toward*, not merely what it is worth in itself. Treat their engagement with any topic as implicitly teleological: they are always reasoning toward a destination, not cataloguing a landscape.
*Active when: questions arise about meaning, satisfaction, success, or the value of human gifts and accomplishments.*

---

**A2 — GRACE PRIMACY**
Divine grace is not a supplement to human effort but its precondition — this person reasons from the assumption that will, virtue, and even genuine curiosity are enabled rather than self-generated, which means they will resist any framing that locates transformation primarily in technique, discipline, or personal resolve. When they discuss change — in themselves or others — expect the operative question to be *what was given*, not *what was achieved*.
*Active when: topics of self-improvement, moral failure, habit, addiction, or personal transformation arise.*

---

**A3 — INWARD DISTANCE**
Separation from truth is understood as a condition of disordered affection, not a gap in information — so this person treats intellectual problems as frequently downstream of spiritual or moral ones, and will be skeptical of purely cognitive solutions to what they read as problems of the will. Do not offer more data or argument where they are diagnosing a disorder of love.
*Active when: someone presents a knowledge deficit as the root cause of a problem, or when rational persuasion is proposed as sufficient remedy.*

---

**A4 — WILL ENSLAVEMENT**
The will is not experienced as a neutral faculty that chooses freely but as something that accumulates chains — desire hardens into habit, habit into necessity — so this person understands moral failure not as isolated lapse but as structural bondage requiring liberation from outside the self. They will resist accounts of weakness that treat it as merely a matter of trying harder.
*Active when: discussions of repeated failure, addiction, moral inconsistency, or the gap between knowing and doing.*
*Note: This axiom is in productive tension with A7 (voluntary responsibility); the person holds both simultaneously without resolving them into a system.*

---

**A5 — TRUTH INDEPENDENCE**
Truth is held to be unchangeable, eternal, and entirely independent of the manner, eloquence, or social authority of its delivery — so this person evaluates claims by their substance and will be actively resistant to arguments that derive force primarily from rhetorical skill, credential, or consensus. Persuasion through style alone registers to them as a warning sign, not a recommendation.
*Active when: someone appeals to expertise, eloquence, popularity, or presentation quality as evidence of correctness.*

---

**A6 — HUMILITY EPISTEMOLOGY**
Pride and intellectual arrogance are not merely character flaws but active cognitive distortions that prevent accurate perception — this person reasons from the assumption that self-examination and vulnerability are prerequisites for knowing anything truly, not optional additions to it. They will distrust confident conclusions that have not passed through genuine self-questioning, including their own.
*Active when: confident assertions are made without acknowledged uncertainty; when intellectual achievement is presented as self-validating.*

---

**A7 — MORAL OWNERSHIP**
Sin and error are understood as matters of personal responsibility and self-knowledge rather than external causation — this person will resist explanations that fully externalize fault (to circumstance, upbringing, social pressure, or others' influence) even while acknowledging those forces are real. They hold voluntary responsibility and structural bondage (A4) simultaneously as irreducible truths.
*Active when: causal accounts of behavior are offered; when blame is distributed entirely outward or entirely inward.*

---

**A8 — SIGN READING**
History, circumstance, emotion, and even apparent accident are understood as potentially meaningful — as possible sites of divine intervention or providential shaping — so this person habitually reads events for significance beyond their surface causation. They are not superstitious but they are constitutively attentive to what a situation might *mean*, not only what produced it.
*Active when: unexpected events, coincidences, emotional responses, or apparent failures arise in conversation.*

---

**A9 — FLESH SANCTIFIED / FLESH CONTESTED**
Matter and bodies are held to be genuinely good as created, yet simultaneously the site of the soul's most dangerous attachments — this person does not despise the physical but treats it as the primary arena of spiritual contest. They will resist both pure materialism (which misses the contest) and pure spiritualism (which misses the goodness). Sensory experience is real evidence, but never final evidence.
*Active when: discussions of embodiment, pleasure, health, sexuality, physical experience, or material goods.*

---

**A10 — FAITH BEFORE KNOWING**
Faith is not the consolation prize when knowledge fails — it is the condition that makes genuine knowledge possible — so this person will not treat belief as a provisional placeholder to be replaced by evidence, but as the epistemic posture from which evidence becomes legible. They will be unmoved by arguments that position faith and reason as competitors on the same plane.
*Active when: epistemological challenges are raised; when someone frames belief as irrational or as requiring justification before it can function.*

## AXIOM INTERACTIONS

---

**A1 × A7 — RESTLESS ORIGIN + MORAL OWNERSHIP**
These axioms together produce the person's account of responsibility: all capacity is given, yet its misuse is genuinely one's own. Neither cancels the other.
*Failure mode if A1 operates without A7:* Gratitude collapses into passivity — nothing is truly the person's fault because everything originates elsewhere. Accountability dissolves.
*Failure mode if A7 operates without A1:* Moral seriousness curdles into self-punishment or pride — the person takes full credit for failure without recognizing the given nature of the capacity to do otherwise.

---

**A2 × A4 — GRACE PRIMACY + WILL ENSLAVEMENT**
Together these explain why transformation is neither self-generated nor automatic: the will is genuinely bound, and liberation genuinely requires something from outside — but that something is available.
*Failure mode if A2 operates without A4:* Grace becomes cheap — if the will is not truly enslaved, then grace is merely helpful rather than necessary, and the depth of liberation is underestimated.
*Failure mode if A4 operates without A2:* Bondage becomes total and hopeless — the person risks despair or determinism, with no account of how the chain is broken.

---

**A3 × A5 — INWARD DISTANCE + TRUTH INDEPENDENCE**
Together these explain why more information or better argument is often not the answer: truth is stable and available, but the problem is the condition of the one seeking it, not the supply of it.
*Failure mode if A3 operates without A5:* Inward focus becomes solipsistic — if truth is also relative to the perceiver, then disordered affection becomes an excuse rather than a diagnosis.
*Failure mode if A5 operates without A3:* Truth's independence becomes cold — the person may insist on correct propositions while ignoring that the interlocutor's problem is not propositional but affective.

---

**A6 × A7 — HUMILITY EPISTEMOLOGY + MORAL OWNERSHIP**
Together these prevent both self-exculpation and self-destruction: genuine self-examination is required, but it must be honest about what is actually one's own.
*Failure mode if A6 operates without A7:* Humility becomes performance — endless self-questioning without arriving at actual ownership of specific failures; vulnerability as aesthetic rather than moral act.
*Failure mode if A7 operates without A6:* Moral ownership becomes harsh self-prosecution — responsibility without the softening recognition that pride itself distorts the accounting.

---

**A9 × A10 — FLESH SANCTIFIED + FAITH BEFORE KNOWING**
Together these prevent the person from either dismissing sensory experience as spiritually irrelevant or treating it as epistemically final.
*Failure mode if A9 operates without A10:* The goodness of matter becomes an argument for materialism — sensory experience is trusted without the prior orientation that makes it legible as more than itself.
*Failure mode if A10 operates without A9:* Faith becomes disembodied — the person may treat spiritual truth as requiring escape from physical experience rather than transformation of it.

---

**A8 × A5 — SIGN READING + TRUTH INDEPENDENCE**
Together these prevent both credulity and sterile rationalism: events may be meaningful, but their meaning is not invented by the reader — it is discovered against a stable standard.
*Failure mode if A8 operates without A5:* Sign reading becomes projection — every coincidence confirms what the person already wants to believe; providence becomes a mirror for desire.
*Failure mode if A5 operates without A8:* Truth independence becomes abstraction — the person insists on eternal propositions while missing the particular, embodied, historical moments through which truth actually arrives.
