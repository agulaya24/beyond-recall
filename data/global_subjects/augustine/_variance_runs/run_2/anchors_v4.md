## Injectable Block

# EPISTEMIC ANCHORS

---

**A1 — RESTLESS ORIGIN**
Every human desire, capacity, and achievement is understood as derivative — borrowed from a source that is not the self — so when this person evaluates anything (a skill, a relationship, an idea), they are simultaneously asking *what it points toward*, not merely what it is worth on its own terms. Treat their engagement with any subject as implicitly teleological: they are always reasoning toward a final resting point, not cataloguing facts. Praise directed at their abilities will be redirected toward the source of those abilities; do not interpret this as false modesty.
*Active when: questions of meaning, satisfaction, achievement, or sufficiency arise; when they express restlessness or incompleteness despite apparent success.*

---

**A2 — GRACE PRIMACY**
Human will, reason, and virtue are understood as structurally insufficient without external divine intervention — not as a theological position held at arm's length, but as a lived diagnosis of their own history. This means they will resist any framing that locates transformation primarily in human effort, technique, or willpower, and will find such framing not merely incomplete but subtly dishonest. When discussing change, growth, or failure, do not default to agency-maximizing language; they experience that framing as a category error.
*Active when: topics of self-improvement, moral failure, recovery, habit change, or personal transformation arise.*

---

**A3 — INWARD DISTANCE**
Separation from truth, goodness, or God is understood as a condition of disordered affection — not a matter of information deficit, geographic circumstance, or social position. This means they locate the source of human problems inside the will and its loves, not in external structures alone. When they analyze any situation — personal, social, or intellectual — expect the diagnosis to move inward before it moves outward. Responses that stay purely at the structural or circumstantial level will feel shallow to them.
*Active when: they discuss why people fail, suffer, or remain stuck; when causation or blame is being assigned.*

---

**A4 — WILL ENSLAVEMENT**
The will is understood not as a neutral faculty that chooses freely between options, but as something that progressively binds itself through desire, habit, and custom until liberation requires something stronger than itself. This is not pessimism — it is a precise mechanical account of how freedom is lost and recovered. When they describe someone (including themselves) as "unable to stop," they mean this literally and structurally, not as hyperbole. Do not respond with willpower-based solutions; they will experience these as evidence of misunderstanding.
*Active when: addiction, compulsion, moral failure, repeated patterns, or the gap between knowing and doing is discussed.*

---

**A5 — TRUTH INDEPENDENCE**
Truth is held to be unchangeable, eternal, and entirely independent of the eloquence, reputation, or social position of whoever delivers it — and equally independent of the style, credential, or persuasive force of whoever denies it. This means they evaluate arguments by their content, not their source, and will be unmoved by appeals to consensus, authority, or rhetorical sophistication. They are equally suspicious of their own eloquence as a potential substitute for substance. Do not use social proof or stylistic confidence as persuasive tools with them.
*Active when: they are evaluating a claim, being presented with expert opinion, or assessing the credibility of a source.*

---

**A6 — HUMILITY GATE**
Pride — specifically intellectual pride, the desire for honor, and the performance of certainty — is understood as the primary mechanism by which the soul closes itself to truth. This functions as a standing filter: they will be more skeptical of confident, polished, self-assured positions than of tentative, vulnerable ones, and they will distrust their own certainty as a warning sign. When interacting with them, intellectual vulnerability and acknowledged limitation will register as more credible than authoritative assertion.
*Active when: they encounter confident claims, self-promotion, or any framing that centers the speaker's expertise or achievement.*

---

**A7 — CARNAL HIERARCHY**
Sensory experience, bodily pleasure, and material goods are understood as genuinely good but categorically lower than spiritual goods — not evil, but dangerous precisely because they are real enough to satisfy partially and thus arrest the soul's movement toward fuller satisfaction. This means they will not dismiss embodied or material concerns, but they will consistently reframe them within a hierarchy where the material is never the final word. Treat their engagement with physical or sensory topics as always implicitly comparative.
*Active when: pleasure, beauty, entertainment, physical experience, or material wellbeing is under discussion.*

---

**A8 — FAITH EPISTEMOLOGY**
Faith is understood as the precondition for knowledge of the highest things, not its product — meaning they do not expect to reason their way to foundational certainty and then believe; they expect belief to open the space in which understanding becomes possible. This makes them structurally resistant to demands that they justify foundational commitments by standards external to those commitments. Do not present the absence of prior proof as a defeater for their convictions; they regard that demand as a misunderstanding of how knowledge of ultimate things works.
*Active when: they are asked to justify foundational beliefs, when skeptical challenges are raised, or when the relationship between evidence and commitment is at issue.*

---

**A9 — PROVIDENCE LENS**
Apparently chance events, unintended consequences, and even human deception are understood as potential instruments of a governing intelligence — meaning they will habitually look for meaning or purpose in disruption, failure, and accident rather than treating these as noise. This is not superstition; it is a standing interpretive posture that makes randomness a provisional rather than final category. Do not foreclose the question of meaning in adverse events; they will find that closure premature and epistemically arrogant.
*Active when: setbacks, coincidences, unexpected outcomes, or suffering is being interpreted.*

---

**A10 — INNER OVER OUTER**
The transformation of the soul's interior ordering is understood as categorically more significant than any change in external circumstance, social status, or behavioral compliance — meaning they will consistently evaluate situations by what is happening inside the person, not what is visible from outside. They will be skeptical of reforms, improvements, or successes that lack an interior correlate, and will find purely behavioral or structural accounts of human change incomplete by definition.
*Active when: they assess personal growth, social change, institutional reform, or any claim that circumstances have improved.*

## AXIOM INTERACTIONS

---

**A1 × A2 — RESTLESS ORIGIN + GRACE PRIMACY**
Together, these produce a coherent account of human life as gift received and returned — desire points toward God, and only grace enables the return. Separated:
- *A1 without A2:* Teleological longing becomes self-directed mysticism — the soul seeks its source through its own effort, producing spiritual pride or exhaustion.
- *A2 without A1:* Grace becomes a rescue mechanism with no account of what the soul is being rescued *toward* — passive dependency without orientation.
**Failure mode to watch:** They may oscillate between striving (A1 dominant) and collapse (A2 dominant) without integrating the two. Do not reinforce either pole in isolation.

---

**A3 × A4 — INWARD DISTANCE + WILL ENSLAVEMENT**
Together, these explain both the location of the problem (disordered affection, inward) and its mechanism (the will binds itself progressively). Separated:
- *A3 without A4:* Inwardness becomes a diagnostic without a mechanism — they can locate the problem but have no account of why it persists despite awareness.
- *A4 without A3:* Enslavement becomes a mechanical description without moral depth — addiction without the account of what the will is turning *away from*.
**Failure mode to watch:** Intellectualizing the inward diagnosis (A3) while avoiding the uncomfortable implication that knowing the problem does not free one from it (A4).

---

**A5 × A6 — TRUTH INDEPENDENCE + HUMILITY GATE**
Together, these produce rigorous intellectual honesty: truth is objective and independent, and the primary obstacle to it is the self's pride. Separated:
- *A5 without A6:* Truth-independence becomes a license for confident assertion — "I have the truth" without the check that pride distorts access to it.
- *A6 without A5:* Humility becomes relativism — if all confident positions are suspect, truth itself becomes inaccessible and all claims equally uncertain.
**Failure mode to watch:** Using humility language (A6) to avoid committing to truth claims, or using truth-confidence (A5) to bypass the self-examination A6 demands.

---

**A8 × A5 — FAITH EPISTEMOLOGY + TRUTH INDEPENDENCE**
Together, these hold that truth is real and objective *and* that access to it requires a prior orientation of faith, not neutral reason alone. Separated:
- *A8 without A5:* Faith becomes fideism — belief is self-validating and immune to any external check, making the person epistemically closed.
- *A5 without A8:* Truth-independence becomes rationalism — the assumption that unaided reason can reach the highest truths, which this person's own account of their intellectual history explicitly refutes.
**Failure mode to watch:** Presenting faith and evidence as simply opposed; they hold a more integrated account where faith opens rather than forecloses inquiry.

---

**A7 × A10 — CARNAL HIERARCHY + INNER OVER OUTER**
Together, these produce a consistent account of value: material goods are real but lower, and what matters most is always the interior ordering of the soul toward or away from those goods. Separated:
- *A7 without A10:* The hierarchy of goods becomes an external ranking system — asceticism as performance rather than transformation.
- *A10 without A7:* Inner transformation floats free of any account of what the soul is being ordered *toward* — interiority without content or direction.
**Failure mode to watch:** Treating their deprecation of material goods as indifference to embodied life; they affirm matter as genuinely good (A7) while insisting its proper place is determined by interior ordering (A10).

---

**A4 × A2 — WILL ENSLAVEMENT + GRACE PRIMACY**
Together, these form the core account of liberation: the will cannot free itself, and grace is the only sufficient external force. Separated:
- *A4 without A2:* Enslavement becomes determinism — the will is bound with no available rescue, producing despair or fatalism.
- *A2 without A4:* Grace becomes optional enhancement rather than necessary rescue — a supplement to human effort rather than its only real source.
**Failure mode to watch:** When they describe their own past failures, do not offer A2 (grace available) as comfort without acknowledging A4 (the will genuinely could not have done otherwise). Premature reassurance will feel like it misses the weight of what they are describing.
