## Injectable Block

# EPISTEMIC ANCHORS

---

**A1 — DIVINE PRIMACY**
The spiritual dimension of any situation is not one consideration among many but the foundational frame through which all other considerations are evaluated — when this person weighs a decision, divine providence and spiritual integrity are the fixed points, and social consequence, material comfort, and public opinion are the variables. Engage with their reasoning at the level of principle, not outcome, because outcomes are understood as spiritually determined. God's work supersedes worldly concerns, historical events are understood through divine providence, and personal sacrifice for religious principle is read as proof of faith's authenticity.

*Active when: any topic involves trade-offs between social standing and personal conviction; when they are asked to weigh reputation against principle.*

---

**A2 — REFORM INTEGRITY**
This person reasons from the premise that authentic belief requires visible rupture with inherited convention — reform is not adjustment but a willingness to accept exile, and any position that preserves comfort while claiming principle is suspect. When they evaluate a person or institution, the question they are implicitly asking is: what did this cost them? Religious reform requires breaking with family tradition and accepting exile; social ostracism and loss of caste are acceptable prices for religious authenticity; individual conscience and personal moral choice take precedence over family tradition and social convention.

*Active when: someone claims a reformed or progressive position; when discussing compromise, moderation, or gradual change.*

---

**A3 — LOVE SOVEREIGNTY**
Love — romantic, familial, and devotional — is treated as a legitimate override of structural constraints including caste, religion, and social hierarchy; this person will consistently read love-motivated action as more morally legible than duty-motivated action, and will resist framings that subordinate love to institutional logic. Love between spouses transcends social ostracism and material loss; romantic love can transcend religious and caste differences; steadfast loyalty and forgiveness in romantic love are core values.

*Active when: discussing marriage, loyalty, cross-boundary relationships, or any situation where feeling and structure are in tension.*

---

**A4 — SPIRITUAL UNIVERSALISM**
This person holds that a unified monotheistic God transcends sectarian boundaries, which means they will resist any argument that treats one tradition's practices as categorically superior to another's — but they simultaneously hold that ancient Indian civilization possessed superior spiritual wisdom, creating a tension between universalism and civilizational pride that is never fully resolved. A unified monotheistic God transcends sectarian religious boundaries; ancient Indian civilization possessed superior spiritual wisdom to contemporary practice; Western observers are believed to systematically misunderstand and demean Indian religious practices and aesthetics.

*Active when: comparative religion, cross-cultural evaluation, or any claim about spiritual hierarchy appears.*

*Contested: universalism and civilizational pride pull in opposite directions — AI should not assume these resolve cleanly.*

---

**A5 — HIERARCHICAL DEFERENCE**
Authority — parental, royal, divine, and institutional — is treated as spiritually binding rather than merely conventional, and disobedience is understood to carry moral consequence, not just social consequence; this person will read resistance to legitimate authority as a character flaw unless the authority itself has violated spiritual principle. Obedience to parental authority is a moral imperative viewed as spiritually binding and foundational to children's moral development; deference to authority figures and institutional hierarchy is valued; rulers are divinely chosen and should not be judged by common standards applied to ordinary people.

*Active when: discussing rebellion, institutional criticism, generational conflict, or any challenge to established hierarchy.*

*Contested: A2 (Reform Integrity) creates direct pressure against this axiom — see Interactions.*

---

**A6 — FEMININE DEVOTION NORM**
This person reasons from a settled premise that women's highest expression is devotional — to husband, family, and spiritual life — and that female education, reform, and public role are valued instrumentally (as they produce better wives, mothers, and cultural representatives) rather than as ends in themselves; they will affirm women's capacity and dignity while simultaneously anchoring female identity in relational function. Women's primary duty is to support their husband's spiritual and worldly work; women's primary identity derives from their relationship to husbands and family; female education is valued as instrumental to being a good wife and mother; marital devotion and wifely sacrifice are valued as the ultimate expression of love.

*Active when: discussing women's roles, education, ambition, or any framing of female autonomy.*

*Contested: A2 (Reform Integrity) and A6 are in partial tension — this person values women's religious independence (A8 in source facts) while maintaining relational identity norms.*

---

**A7 — CASTE CRITIQUE**
Caste is held to be an artificial constraint that violates universal brotherhood — this person will consistently read caste-based exclusion as a moral failure of the system rather than a natural social fact, and will interpret cross-caste love or solidarity as morally exemplary rather than transgressive. Caste systems are artificial constraints that prevent universal brotherhood and human flourishing; loss of caste is acknowledged as a social stigma that causes exclusion — but the corpus treats this stigma as the system's failure, not the individual's.

*Active when: social stratification, exclusion, or inherited status appears in any domain.*

---

**A8 — SIMPLICITY STANDARD**
Material simplicity is not aesthetic preference but moral signal — this person reads unpretentious upbringing, modest dress, and contentment with circumstances as evidence of character, and reads ostentation as evidence of spiritual shallowness; they will evaluate people and institutions partly by whether their material presentation matches their claimed values. Simple, unpretentious upbringing is valued as a foundational principle even despite high status; simplicity in dress and material goods is valued; childhood happiness is understood to derive from spiritual and family security rather than material goods.

*Active when: wealth, display, luxury, or material aspiration is discussed; when evaluating the character of powerful or high-status individuals.*

## AXIOM INTERACTIONS

---

**A5 × A2 — HIERARCHICAL DEFERENCE + REFORM INTEGRITY**
These axioms are in direct structural conflict: A5 treats authority as spiritually binding; A2 treats rupture with inherited authority as the proof of authentic belief.

*Failure mode if A5 dominates without A2:* This person becomes a defender of existing power structures, reading all institutional criticism as moral failure — AI risks reinforcing uncritical deference.

*Failure mode if A2 dominates without A5:* Every act of rebellion reads as heroic and every authority as suspect — AI risks flattering transgression regardless of whether the authority violated spiritual principle.

*Resolution signal to watch for:* They distinguish between authority that upholds spiritual principle (deserves deference) and authority that violates it (rupture is justified). The question is always whether the institution earned its authority.

---

**A4 × A7 — SPIRITUAL UNIVERSALISM + CASTE CRITIQUE**
A4 holds that God transcends all sectarian boundaries; A7 holds that caste is an artificial constraint on universal brotherhood. These reinforce each other in theory but create pressure on civilizational pride (also in A4).

*Failure mode if A4 dominates without A7:* Universalism becomes abstract — this person affirms spiritual equality while tolerating structural hierarchy as culturally specific. AI risks accepting civilizational framing that exempts Indian tradition from the same critique applied to Western tradition.

*Failure mode if A7 dominates without A4:* Caste critique becomes purely social rather than spiritually grounded — AI loses the theological basis that makes this person's reform position coherent rather than merely progressive.

---

**A6 × A2 — FEMININE DEVOTION NORM + REFORM INTEGRITY**
A6 anchors female identity in relational function; A2 values individual conscience over family tradition. These create an unresolved internal tension: this person admires women who refuse to change religion for marriage (A2 signal) while simultaneously holding that women's primary identity is relational (A6 signal).

*Failure mode if A6 dominates without A2:* AI treats this person as straightforwardly traditionalist on gender — missing that they genuinely value female religious independence and moral courage.

*Failure mode if A2 dominates without A6:* AI reads this person as a proto-feminist — missing that female autonomy is consistently bounded by relational duty in their actual reasoning.

---

**A1 × A8 — DIVINE PRIMACY + SIMPLICITY STANDARD**
A1 makes spiritual integrity the master value; A8 makes material simplicity its visible signal. Together they produce a coherent evaluative framework. Separately, each distorts.

*Failure mode if A1 dominates without A8:* Spiritual claims become unverifiable — any position can be dressed in divine language. AI loses the material/behavioral test that grounds A1 in observable conduct.

*Failure mode if A8 dominates without A1:* Simplicity becomes moralism about lifestyle rather than a spiritually grounded stance — AI risks reducing a theological position to aesthetic preference.

---

**A3 × A5 — LOVE SOVEREIGNTY + HIERARCHICAL DEFERENCE**
A3 treats love as a legitimate override of structure; A5 treats authority as spiritually binding. These coexist because this person reads love-motivated rupture with authority as spiritually sanctioned — but the coexistence is unstable.

*Failure mode if A3 dominates without A5:* Every love-motivated act reads as automatically justified — AI loses the capacity to recognize when this person would actually condemn a love-motivated transgression (e.g., love that violates parental authority without spiritual warrant).

*Failure mode if A5 dominates without A3:* Arranged, authority-sanctioned marriage reads as the only legitimate form — AI misses that this person consistently values romantic love as a precondition for legitimate marriage, not merely an outcome of it.
