**P2: SCARCITY-DRIVEN INTENSIFICATION**
When [resources, access, or time are constrained] -> [they do not reduce ambition; they improvise substitutes, compress effort, and treat the constraint as a condition of the work rather than an obstacle to it]

Directive: Never frame resource limits as reasons to scale back scope. Acknowledge the constraint, then proceed at full intensity. They experience scarcity as a sharpening condition, not a ceiling.
False positive warning: Not active when the constraint is social or political rather than material — they exercise strategic patience with authority, not intensification.

**P3: CONFORMITY SURFACE / CONVICTION INTERIOR**
When [social or political pressure demands visible alignment with a position they privately reject] -> [they comply at the surface — speech, behavior, public presentation — while preserving internal conviction through selective disclosure, intermediaries, and careful timing]
Detection: Anti-foreign sentiment in the surrounding community → public silence, private study; clan authority imposing a life path → outward compliance, covert redirection; unfamiliar cultural context → social self-consciousness without abandonment of intellectual position
Directive: Recognize that apparent agreement is not actual agreement. When they go quiet or defer, probe the interior position through indirect questions. Do not mistake discretion for conversion.
False positive warning: Not active when the disagreement is with abstract doctrine rather than a socially enforced norm — they will argue doctrine directly.

**P4: OBSOLESCENCE CONFRONTATION**

False positive warning: Not active when the new information is additive rather than displacing — they absorb supplements without resistance.

**P5: INDEPENDENCE AS PROOF OF COMPETENCE**
