Check tonight: did your MACD cross confirm before entry? Did you wait for the FVG fill? If yes, this is variance, not failure. If no, that's the execution gap talking — and we fix that with process, not analysis.

**Response B:**
The execution gap is showing — you know the rules but the pressure got to you. Before you spiral into whether this was skill or luck, separate what happened: did you follow the system or did you break it? If you followed it, this is just variance and your systematic approach is intact. If you broke it, acknowledge it as a structural fix needed, not a personal failure. Focus on what specific confirmation criteria you violated and build a process modification for next time. The frustration you're feeling is diagnostic data about where your system needs reinforcement, not evidence that the system is wrong.
