## CRITICAL ISSUES
The main critical issue is the need for further testing to confirm the generalization of the results to living users and to other tasks beyond passage-level prediction.

## MINOR ISSUES
Minor issues include the potential for prompt-template-induced hedging in the Base Layer retrieval results and the need for optimizing the prompt template to reduce this effect.

## VERDICT
The paper presents a well-argued case for the importance of behavioral specification in AI personalization, with a clear distinction between recall and representational accuracy, and provides evidence for the effectiveness of the proposed approach, but requires further testing to fully confirm its claims.
