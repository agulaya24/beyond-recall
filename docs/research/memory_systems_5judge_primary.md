## Method

- **Primary panel (5 judges):** Haiku 4.5, Sonnet 4.6, Opus 4.6, GPT-4o, GPT-5.4.
- **7-judge sensitivity:** adds Gemini Flash and (where available) Gemini Pro.
- Per (subject, condition, judge): mean across questions.
- Per (subject, condition): mean across judges in panel.
- Aggregate: mean across subjects (subject = unit of inference).
- Δ_spec = mean(C3) − mean(C1) per subject, then mean across subjects.
- Low-baseline slice: 9 subjects with 5-judge C5 ≤ 2.0 (ebers, sunity_devee, hamerton, fukuzawa, bernal_diaz, babur, seacole, keckley, yung_wing).

**Base Layer Native:** No data. Base Layer IS the authored pipeline; there is no separate `baselayer_fullpipeline_*` variant. BL appears only in the controlled column.
